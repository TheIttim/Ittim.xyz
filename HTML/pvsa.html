Presidental Volunteer Service Award

The PVSA is awarded to volunteers in the United States, categorized by age, and number of hours served in a 12-month timeframe, or a lifetime. Volunteers must then go through a "Certifying Organization" that will request and turn in all the necessary paperwork, then hand out the award earned. For me that is SkillsUSA, and more about their process can be found here.
